Pondbell's future

Pondbell has come far but it doesnt plan on stopping any time soon. This story has placed #37 in the Elven category on Wattpad thats out of #663 of the stories in that same category. #661 in the Mental Illness category out of #11.1k . ADHD is #248 out of #3.5k . And a whopping #865 in the fashion category out of #17.8k and those ranks are steadily growing every day. This book will continue to grow in popularity and because of this I am proud to announce that the story will be part of a franchise, there will be no less than five other books. That isn't all however, I am incredibly excited to say that this book will be getting a graphic novel adaptation. My team and I are working diligently to get this graphic novel out and ready for readers.
