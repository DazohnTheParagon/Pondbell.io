Where Pondbell came from. What it is. What it plans to be.

A book written by Dazohn Reid

Synopsis

Pondbell follows a young adult named Naira Ayad. She is a middle eastern woman with a heart of gold. She lives in a world where people live alongside Others. Others are species such as Therians, Vampire, Merfolk and other fantastical beings. This has become quite commonplace in this society but there is without a doubt still tension between the species. Naira meets a young man named Credence and he changes her life forever.
